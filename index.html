Recent projects

Fixing the Church roof

Our recent project involved the restoration of a historic church roof. The church, a symbol of the community's rich history and faith, had been weathered by time and elements. The roof, in particular, had suffered significant damage, with missing tiles and a weakened structure that threatened the integrity of the entire building.

The restoration project was a delicate task. We aimed to preserve the original architecture while ensuring the roof's durability and longevity. Our team of skilled craftsmen used traditional methods in combination with modern materials to restore the roof to its former glory. The project was a success, with the church roof not only regaining its original charm but also ready to withstand the test of time.

Restoration of the stonework

Another significant part of our restoration project was focused on the church's stonework. Over the years, the stone had weathered and eroded, losing much of its original detail and beauty. Our goal was to restore the stonework to its original state, preserving the church's historical and architectural value.

Our team of experienced stonemasons undertook the task with great care and precision. They used a combination of traditional techniques and modern technology to meticulously restore each stone. The result was a stunning transformation that brought the church's intricate stonework back to life, enhancing the overall aesthetic of the building and ensuring its preservation for future generations.

Renewal of the Church Doors

The church doors, serving as the gateway to this sacred place, were also a crucial part of our restoration project. Over centuries, these doors had become worn and weathered, losing their original grandeur. Our objective was to renew these doors while maintaining their historical significance.

Our team of experts carefully refurbished the doors, paying close attention to the intricate carvings and details that make them unique. We used a blend of traditional craftsmanship and modern techniques to ensure the doors were restored to their original state, while also improving their durability. The renewed doors now stand as a testament to the church's rich history, welcoming parishioners and visitors alike with their renewed beauty.

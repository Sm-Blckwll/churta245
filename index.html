About Us

The Friends of St. Nectan's, Stoke, is an independent secular organization established in June 2002. Our objective is to promote or assist for the public benefit the preservation, repair, maintenance, improvement, and ornamentation of the fabric and furnishings of the Church of St. Nectan's, Stoke, the Parish Church of Hartland.

Our organization is run entirely by volunteers, ensuring the most effective use of funds received. Membership is open to all, offering an ideal opportunity for both local residents and holiday visitors to stay engaged and connected with Hartland.

The Friends of St. Nectan's, Stoke, raises funds through subscriptions, donations, legacies, and fundraising events, sustaining our mission to safeguard our community's rich history. If you share our belief in the importance of preserving the past to inform the future, ensuring that each restoration project is executed with the utmost care and attention to detail, please consider donating.

Recent projects

Refurbishment of the organ

One of our recent projects involved the restoration of the historic church organ. It was a momentous endeavour that breathed new life into the heart of the congregation's worship space. With meticulous care and attention to detail, the skilled artisans restored the instrument to its former glory, while honouring its rich history. Each pipe was lovingly cleaned and tuned, with every mechanism inspected. The majestic sound of the organ now once again fills the hall.

Aditionally, the refurbishment project also introduced eight new organ stops, each carefully selected to enhance the instrument's versatility and musical expression. These new additions expanded the tonal palette of the organ, allowing for a wider range of sounds to accompany the congregation in their worship and to resonate through the sanctuary with power and beauty.

Restoration of the West door

In a recent project aimed at preserving the structural integrity and historical significance of our church, we focused on repairing the West door and stonework. With careful craftsmanship and attention to detail, we addressed weathered elements that had withstood the test of time. Each stone was meticulously restored, ensuring that the intricate details and architectural beauty of the entrance were preserved for generations to come.

The repairs to the West door and stonework not only safeguarded the physical foundation of our church but also upheld its symbolic importance within the community. Through the dedication of skilled artisans, we honoured the craftsmanship of those who came before us, maintaining a connection to the rich history and traditions of our sacred space. As the repaired entrance stands renewed, it serves as a beacon of resilience and continuity, welcoming all who enter with a sense of reverence and belonging.

In undertaking these repairs, we reaffirmed our commitment to stewardship and preservation, recognizing the West door and stonework as integral components of our church's identity. Through our efforts, we ensure that future generations will continue to be inspired by the enduring legacy of faith and craftsmanship that defines our beloved sanctuary.

Renewal of the carpets

In a recent project dedicated to preserving the sanctity of our worship space, we embarked on refurbishing the flooring and caring for all fabrics within the church. With careful attention, worn carpets were replaced, unveiling refreshed patterns and colours that rejuvenated the ambiance of our sanctuary. Additionally, every fabric, from altar draperies to wall tapestries, underwent meticulous cleansing and arrangement, ensuring a renewed sense of elegance and reverence throughout.

We breathed new life into the very fibres that uphold the spiritual tapestry of our church. Vibrant hues and intricate patterns emerged on once-faded floors, while delicate fabrics were tenderly cleansed and arranged to restore their lustre. In upholding the sanctity of our worship space, we affirm our dedication to fostering an atmosphere conducive to spiritual reflection and devotion.

In honouring the legacy of our sacred space, we recognize the importance of every element, from the grandeur of the carpets to the softness of the fabrics. Our endeavour reflects a timeless commitment to preserving the enduring beauty of faith and devotion, ensuring that our sanctuary remains a haven for spiritual nourishment and reverence.
